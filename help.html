Change or reset a password

You can change your password from the account settings page on the website. You only need to make the change once and when you do, it will automatically apply to any computers or mobile devices linked to your account.

Forgot your password?

When using the desktop application, you only need your password when you first install the desktop application or whenever you link or relink your computer to your account. Control all access to your account via the Security.

Change your password from the website
  • 1 Sign in to the Dropbox website
  • 2 Click on your name at the top of any page to open the account menu and select Settings
  • 3 Select the Security tab
  • 4 In the Password section, click the Change password link
